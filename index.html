Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common dental queries and treatment details

What are the clinic timings?
We are open Monday to Saturday from 11:00 AM to 2:00 PM and 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM. Sunday is closed.
Is Root Canal Treatment (RCT) painful?
With modern anesthesia and advanced techniques, RCT is virtually painless. It actually relieves the pain caused by tooth decay.
How long do dental braces treatments take?
The duration of orthodontic treatment varies depending on complexity, typically ranging from 12 to 24 months.
Do you provide emergency dental services?
Yes, we accommodate same-day emergency appointments for severe pain, tooth fractures, or swelling.
What is the cost of dental implants?
Dental implants are highly customized. The cost depends on the brand, material, and number of implants required. We offer affordable premium solutions.
Can I use the Symptom Checker to book directly?
Yes! Simply click on your matching symptom card above. You will receive an instant recommendation and a direct 'Book Recommendation' button that pre-fills the treatment in our online booking form.
Is online appointment booking available 24/7?
Yes, our online booking form is available around the clock. You can submit your appointment request any time. Our team will confirm your slot during clinic hours.
